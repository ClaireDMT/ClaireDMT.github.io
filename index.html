About me

After completing my Master's in Management, I ventured into the IT world as a Consultant. The unknowns of the field were incredibly exciting.

My interest in digital products grew during a global CRM deployment (Salesforce) in the Health Industry.

I then joined MonDocteur.fr and later Doctolib, where I worked as an Onboarding Manager, SaaS Integrator, and Trainer, developing products that empower people.

Wanting to get closer to product development, I joined Le Wagon bootcamp. This experience sparked my passion for programming, and I've been hooked ever since!
